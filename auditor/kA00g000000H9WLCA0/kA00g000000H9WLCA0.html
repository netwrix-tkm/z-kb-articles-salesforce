How do I use SMS for alerts?
You can configure Netwrix Auditor to send alerts to special SMS-enabled emails.

Here is the list of formats/carriers:
  • AT&T - cellnumber@txt.att.net
  • Verizon - cellnumber@vtext.com
  • T-Mobile - cellnumber@tmomail.net
  • Sprint PCS - cellnumber@messaging.sprintpcs.com
  • Virgin Mobile - cellnumber@vmobl.com
  • US Cellular - cellnumber@email.uscc.net
  • Nextel - cellnumber@messaging.nextel.com
  • Boost - cellnumber@myboostmobile.com
  • Alltel - cellnumber@message.alltel.com
  • SimpleMobile - cellnumber@mmst5.tracfone.com
For Canada:
  • Bell - cellnumber@txt.bell.ca
  • Rogers - cellnumber@pcs.rogers.com
  • Telus: msg.telus.com
