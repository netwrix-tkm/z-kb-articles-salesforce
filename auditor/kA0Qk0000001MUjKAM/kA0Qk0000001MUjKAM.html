Overview

To collect data from Exchange Online and Entra ID and create Activity Records, Netwrix Auditor (NA) uses PowerShell cmdlets. It allows to submit commands to Exchange Online and Entra ID over the Modern Authentication app to collect relevant data. Refer to the following list of PowerShell cmdlets that NA uses:

  • Import-Module

  • New-PSSessionOption

  • Connect-ExchangeOnline

  • Update-TypeData

  • Get-Mailbox

  • Get-Group

  • Get-SecurityPrincipal

  • Get-MailboxPermission

  • Disconnect-ExchangeOnline

  • Get-DistributionGroup

  • Get-DynamicDistributionGroup

  • Get-ExecutionPolicy –List

  • Set-ExecutionPolicy -Scope Process Bypass –Force

  • Get-RecipientPermission

  • Get-User

  • Get-Recipient

  • Get-DistributionGroupMember

  • Get-RoleGroupMember

  • Get-EXORecipient

  • Update-Module

  • Get-Module

  • Install-Module

  • Get-PackageProvider

  • Install-PackageProvider

  • Import-PackageProvider

  • Register-PSRepository
