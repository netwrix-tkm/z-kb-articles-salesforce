Symptom

A monitoring plan in Netwrix Auditor shows the Not responding status.

When drilling down to the Details, the following error appears:

One of the Netwrix Auditor services is not responding. Navigate to the Services snap-in and check the service statuses.

Cause

This error indicates that a Netwrix Auditor monitoring plan stopped because data collection timed out prematurely.

Resolution

To prevent premature timeout of data collection, you need to restart some Netwrix services:

  1. On the computer that hosts your Auditor Server, open the Services snap-in.

  2. Locate and restart the following services depending on your audited data source:

    • For all data sources: Netwrix Auditor Data Collection Service

    • For a specific data source: Netwrix Auditor for {Your Data Source} Audit Service

      where {Your Data Source} is the name of the source, such as Active Directory.
