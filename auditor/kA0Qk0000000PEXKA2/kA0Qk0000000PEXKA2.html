Question

Why I do not see any events in the Netwrix Auditor Health Log?

Answer

The issue occurs due to Windows Registry misconfiguration.

To resolve the issue, follow the steps below:

  1. On the Auditor Server, open the Registry Editor.

  2. In the Registry Editor, navigate to the following path: HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\ControlSet001\Services\EventLog\Application\Windows Server Audit Service.

  3. Backup the keys and then remove them completely.

  4. Restart the computer that hosts the Auditor Server.

In Netwrix Auditor, navigate to Health Status dashboard and click the Health log tile to make sure the events have been uploaded.
