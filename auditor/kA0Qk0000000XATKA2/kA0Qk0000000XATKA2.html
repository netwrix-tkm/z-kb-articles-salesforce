Symptom

Email notifications and reports from Netwrix Password Expiration Notifier (PEN) are missing.

Cause

The Display the following From address in email notifications option is enabled for a service account with insufficient permissions.

Resolutions

  • Disable the Display the following From address in email notifications option in PEN:

    1. Launch Password Expiration Notifier, select the affected monitoring plan, and click Edit.

    2. In the Notifications tab, uncheck the Display the following From address in email notifications checkbox, and click Save.

  • Grant the appropriate Send As or Send on Behalf permissions to the service account sending emails in Password Expiration Notifier.
