Answer

  • Prior to the upgrade, stop Netwrix Auditor Archive Service and Netwrix Auditor Management Service in your Netwrix Auditor server. Refer to the following commands to run in elevated PowerShell: 

    Stop-Service -DisplayName "Netwrix Auditor Archive Service"
Stop-Service -DisplayName "Netwrix Auditor Management Service"

  • Once stopped, proceed with the upgrade procedure as recommended by Microsoft.

  • After the upgrade is complete, start the previously stopped services in your Netwrix Auditor server. You can use the following commands in elevated PowerShell: 

    Start-Service -DisplayName "Netwrix Auditor Archive Service"
Start-Service -DisplayName "Netwrix Auditor Management Service"
