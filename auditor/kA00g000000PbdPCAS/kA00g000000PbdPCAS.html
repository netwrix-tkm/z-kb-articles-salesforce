How can I activate or change the Netwrix Auditor license?
To activate or change your license, perform the following steps:
  1. Select Start -> All Programs -> NetWrix -> Netwrix Auditor.
  2. In the left pane, expand Managed Objects --> <your_Managed_Object> node.
  3. Right-click  <your_Managed_Object> node and select About.
  4. Click Change License, enter your company name, license count and code (it is recommended to copy-paste them to avoid mistakes).
  5. Click OK.
You can also review, add and remove licenses under Settings -> Licenses.
