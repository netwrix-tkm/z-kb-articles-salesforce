Question

What's the OpenSSL version used in Netwrix Auditor?

Answer

Netwrix Auditor uses OpenSSL to send emails. To establish the OpenSSL version, review Properties > Details of libeay32.dll located in %NA_installation_path%\Data Collection Core\. As of Netwrix Auditor v10.6.12299, the OpenSSL version used is v1.1.1k.
