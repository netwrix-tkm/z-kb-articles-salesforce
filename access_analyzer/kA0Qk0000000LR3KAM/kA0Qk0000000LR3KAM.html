Symptom

CSV attachments are missing in email reports in Netwrix Enterprise Auditor v11.6.0.45 and older builds.

Cause

This is a known issue that has been fixed in Netwrix Enterprise Auditor v11.6.0.46.

Resoltuion

Upgrade your Enterprise Auditor instance to version v11.6.0.46 or later. You can download the latest version at My Products · Netwrix.
