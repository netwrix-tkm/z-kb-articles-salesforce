To remove conceptClassifier for SharePoint cleanly you must perform two steps:
  1. Roll Back the installation using the conceptClassifier Wizard
  2. Run the conceptClassifier SETUP utility and select the “Remove” option.
To remove conceptClassifier cleanly it is important to perform these two steps in the order shown otherwise all of the facilities installed may not be removed completely.
DO NOT remove the conceptClassifier Solution from SharePoint Central Admin.
