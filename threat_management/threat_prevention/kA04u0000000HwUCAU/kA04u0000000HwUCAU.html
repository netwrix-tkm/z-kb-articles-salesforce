1. Open the StealthINTERCEPT Console.
  2. In the Policy Center on the left side of the console, click on Agents.
  3. Along the top bar of the Agents screen click on Configure Auto Deploy.
  4. Ensure the checkbox for Enable Auto Deploy is checked to enable the form controls.
  5. Enter a fake username and password into the credential fields (i.e., an account that doesn't exist).
  6. Click Apply.
  7. Uncheck the Enable Auto Deploy checkbox.
  8. Click Apply.
