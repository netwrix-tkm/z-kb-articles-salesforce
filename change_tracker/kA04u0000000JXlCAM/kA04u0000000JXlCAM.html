You can download our software from www.nntws.com by first becoming a member through our members area.

 

 




When you click “Become a Member” you will be prompted to fill out the questionnaire below. Keep in mind that once you click sign up, you will need to be approved from our end.

 










Once you have been approved, you will need to log into our members area, and follow the following path - NNT > Members > Member Downloads > Trial Software to Download.

If you have purchased the software, once the installation has completed, you will need to upload a license to you NNT server.


To apply this License, you can go to Settings > On the left side, find the “License” navigation button, and copy and paste the contents into the text box.
