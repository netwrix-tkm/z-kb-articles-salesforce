This article will give you a step by step guide on how to review changes made on your Change Tracker Console whether it be devices going offline, users on Change Tracker making modifications, the deletion of Configuration Templates, etc.

Step-by-step guide

  • Login to Change Tracker.
  • On the top left corner of the page, select the following filter:[No Group Filter]
  • Head into the Events page.
  • Press on the Filters option.
  • Set the following parameters and then press on the apply button: Start date/time, Event type: Audit and Change type: all.
