This NNT how-to article covers the enablement of NNT's File Approved-Safe technology(FAST). Your FAST component is enabled via the NNT Change Tracker license and so will be delivered by NNT on receipt of an order.

The FAST configuration is relatively simple, however there are a couple of key pieces of information which you must be aware of.

For FAST, your NNT Change Tracker must be version7.0.1.51 or above.

View your Change Tracker version by logging into Change Tracker and scrolling to the bottom of the page.

The FAST service communicates with the NNT Cloud on port 8474

The actual address used for FAST is -https://fast.nntsupport.com:8474

Ensure that the NNT Change Tracker server is able to access this address over port 8474.

The communication to this address is always initiated from your Change Tracker server, fast.nntsupport.com will never attempt an unsolicited communication to your Change Tracker installation.

Step-by-step guide

Configure the FAST License:

  • Confirm your Change Tracker version as directed above.
  • Confirm the NNT Change Tracker server can communicate withhttps://fast.nntsupport.com:8474
  • Log into NNT Change Tracker's web console with an admin level account.
  • Move to the 'Settings' tile and scroll down to 'License'
  • Paste your FAST enabled licence from NNT into 'Paste License Text'. You will be asked to log out. Once logged back in, your license details will look something like the below.

Configure the FAST Planned Change:

  • Move to'Settings' and 'Planned Changes'.
