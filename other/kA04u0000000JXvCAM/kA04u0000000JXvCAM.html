Correlog's NNT License Process

The process for Correlog License distribution is outlined below and from now on, Correlog will send all keys to support@nntws.com.

  • NNT will issue an individual PO number for every customer purchase
  • That number will then be traceable as it relates to corresponding payments made or indeed due
  • Corrrelog will issue NNT a 75 day license upon order, which will expire midweek in order to avoid a weekend expiration, which we know can catch us out
  • Only orders placed by NNT Finance or Mark Kerrison/Kedgley will be accepted by Correlog
  • NNT will make regular monthly payments to Correlog listing the customers and PO numbers that the payments relate to on or before the 10th day of each month
  • As soon as payment is received, Correlog will release a full or subscription license where required
