Summary: Textsearch DC removes spaces in loaded data; job connection errors.
Issue/Request: When the audited document contained multiple spaces between text, they were reduced to a single space in the job's results. This resulted in issues for the client when matching data. Turkish language environments were not honoring SQL credentials.
Fix/Enhancement: Now multiple spaces are being respected and reflected in the job's results. Turkish-language environments can connect to SQL with correct credentials.
