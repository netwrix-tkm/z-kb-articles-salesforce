Summary: SMPC-4923: AIC exported report format changes . Issue/Current behavior: Exporting Permissions and Effective Access reports to Excel format was aligning columns to center and the rights columns were showing a 1 instead of check marks. . Fix/Enhancement: The excel exports now show the trustee type, name, and paths columns left justified, and the rights columns show a check mark.
