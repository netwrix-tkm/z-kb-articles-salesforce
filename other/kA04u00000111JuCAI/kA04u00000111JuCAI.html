Symptom

After the recent upgrade, Password Policy Enforcer (PPE) users do not see any password policy requirements.

Causes

One or more of the following causes may affect your PPE installation. Refer to the list of possible causes:

  1. The PPE upgrade is not completed.

  2. The PPE integration with third-party credential providers is misconfigured (e.g., Duo Security, Global Protect, etc.).

  3. Firewall rules for PPE are missing or misconfigured.

  4. In PPE v10.2 and newer, there's a protocol mismatch in PPE Client and Server.
