Summary: SI Licensing. FS lockdown policy license requires a Exchange lockdown policy license SI Licensing. GPO lockdown policy license requires a AD lockdown policy license

Issue: Summary:
SI Licensing. FS lockdown policy license requires a Exchange lockdown policy license
SI Licensing. GPO lockdown policy license requires a AD lockdown policy license
Before fix:  field "Code" in event registration type for FS lockdown events and Exchange lockdown has the same value. The same was for GPO lockdown policy AD lockdown event type. Due to incorrect registration values events were filtered on EM side by license rules.
After fix: "Code" values were changed to unique for every mentioned event type.
