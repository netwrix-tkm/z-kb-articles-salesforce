Instructions: 1. Exit SA Console
2. Navigate to the 0. Collection Group and open the Exceptions Job folder.
3. Replace the DataAnalysis.xml and Job xml files
4. Navigate to the 2.High Risk Sites Group and open the Open Access Job
5. Repeat step 3
6. Note the configured groups need to match the "Name" column from SA_SPAA_Trustees table verbatim. Those names can sometimes be saved as NT Style, a normal string, or claims based (for example "i:0#.w|qa\user" without quotations)
