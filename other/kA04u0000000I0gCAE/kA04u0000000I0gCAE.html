Summary:

SI-4450 60442 - After patching lsass crash when SI agent starts

I-7355 SUVP 2021-07| Kerberos auth. events may be lost or incomplete after applying MSFT 2021-07 KBs

I-7351 Add support for Exchange 2016 CU21 and 2019 CU10

Behavior before Fix:

SI-4450:

HandleTGSRequest was resolved by Windows 2k16-specific signature of HandleTGSRequest which is the signature of different function on 2k19. As the result other function was hooked instead of HandleTGSRequest.

I-7355

SI Authentication monitoring, lockdown and analytics polices may stop working for Kerberos.

Kerberos events may be lost or incomplete.

I-7351

SI Agents do not support Exchange 2016 CU21 and Exchange 2019 CU10

Behavior after Fix:

SI-4450:

Windows 2k16 uses 2k16-specific signatures & 2k19 - 2k19-specific signatures of HandleTGSRequest. Additional 2k19 signature was produced for HandleTGSRequest.

I-7355:

kerberos functionality restored

I-7351:

Extend signatures to support Exchange 2016 CU21 and Exchange 2019 CU10
