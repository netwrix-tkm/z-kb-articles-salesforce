Summary: SharePoint views do not work with HTTPS URLs

Issue: Summary:
27433 - SharePoint views do not work with HTTPS URLs
Behavior Before Fix:
We were failing to fill the SA_SPDLP_MatchesView and SA_SPDLP_MatchHitsView with proper https data in the FilePath columns. When https data was scanned, we always filled the view with a hard-coded 'http' and were missing the hostname.
Behavior After Fix:
Views correctly updated with the correct data, http or https and the correct hostname.
