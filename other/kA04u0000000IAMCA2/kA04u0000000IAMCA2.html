Summary:

SI-4337 57055 - GPO Changes Monitoring Reporting "GPO Untranslated"

Behavior before Fix:

There was no mapping for content of 'fdeploy.ini' & 'fdeploy1.ini' implemented which resulted in GPO service was completely unaware how to interpret file system redirection-related changes.

Behavior after Fix:

Mapping files were created for 'fdeploy.ini' & 'fdeploy1.ini'. For now mapping is supported for 'version', 'flags' , 'Folder_Redirection' & 'FullPath' items.
