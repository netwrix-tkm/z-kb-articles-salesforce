Summary: Resolves issue with SPAA scans that were returning the following error: Unable to enumerate iSharePointFile children of iSharePointFolder, but ended up introducing a bug into customer environments.

Issue: Summary:
This Hotfix is designed to replace HF_8.1_111 which introduced was supposed to resolve a Copy/Paste error, SPAA scans were returning the following error: Unable to enumerate iSharePointFile children of iSharePointFolder, but ended up introducing a bug into customer environments.

This Hotfix is to replace HF_8.1_111 and also includes the ability to choose whether or not match hits are stored during SPSEEK scans.
 
Behavior After Fix:
SPAA scans no longer return this error, customers have the ability to choose whether or not to store match hits from sdd scans.
