Issue: Summary:
I-6165 Exchange | Add support for Exchange 2016 CU16 and 2019 CU5 (release 03/17/20)
I-6170: 2016 & 2019 Servers | No NTLM events; Kerberos events may be lost or incomplete after applying MS Updates, released 03/17/20
Behavior before Fix:
I-6165: SI Agent do not support Exchange 2016 CU16 and 2019 CU5
I-6170: Kerberos events are incomplete due to signature changes in HandleTGSRequest (Windows Server 2019) and KdcVerifyKdcRequest (Windows Server 2016/2019). NTLM events are not coming due to signature change in NlpLogonSamLogon
Behavior after Fix:
I-6165: Extend signature file to support Exchange 2016 CU16 and 2019 CU5
I-6170: Signatures updated, both Kerberos and NTLM must be fully functional.
