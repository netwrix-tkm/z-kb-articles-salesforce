Summary: There have been major revisions to how hub metrics are calculated in StealthAUDIT.

Issue: the way that the metrics dc calculates hub metrics has changed starting with the following versions: 7.0 SP2, 7.1 SP1 and 7.2

the old numbers were not accurate when compared to a user's inbox.

the new numbers should be an accurate match.

therefore, customers may see a significant change in their hub metrics after an upgrade to one of the above versions or later

Instructions: upgrade StealthAUDIT to fix the metrics counts

the explanation for this is:

there was a slight error in the previous calculation method.  
while the error was relatively small, it can add up significantly.  

the fortunate thing is that while there may have been an issue with the numbers being exact previously, they would still be consistent to themselves, and reports would still provide a good measure of hub server utilization, balance, internal external, etc.
