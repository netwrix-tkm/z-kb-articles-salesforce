Interactive logon types often times mean that a login\password window is used to log in to system, but it might not always be the case. Netwrix Auditor relies on Windows Event logs to collect information about logons.
Information about a single logon is spread into 3 or more event records which often times don't go one after another and can have a very large temporal spread. Sometimes, Non-Interactive logons can be recorded in Netwrix Auditor as interactive ones, due to similarities of Event Log recordings in both scenarios where interactive or non-interactive logons are recorded. In this case, we recommend looking at the properties of an account making a logon to see whether it has permission at all to logon interactively
